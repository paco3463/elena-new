One Mission

Turning compassion into action — one child at a time.

The Elena A. Miranda Foundation, founded by Carlos Fausto Miranda, helps children whose families can't afford life-saving medical care. Recently, the Foundation pledged $500,000 to Nicklaus Children's Hospital, which named a new wing of its lobby the "Elena A. Miranda Marketplace" in its honor. With full transparency and zero admin fees, we have one goal: to give every child a fighting chance.

How We Help

Care for the children who need it most

We step in when a life-saving treatment exists, but a family simply can't reach it — and no public funds remain.

Life-Saving Care

Families First

A message from our founder

In honor of Elena.

The Foundation was created as a labor of love, inspired by and honoring the late Elena Andreina Miranda-Serafin. It exists for the children society leaves behind — and for the families who need help the most.

“No child deserves to suffer, and no family deserves to live their child's suffering. Our purpose is simple: to help those incapable of helping themselves.”

— Carlos Fausto Miranda, Founder
